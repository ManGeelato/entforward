Residential cleaning

We specialises in high access cleaning services for the hard to reach places such as windows, ceilings and roofs. All our cleaning staff are fully qualified and follow high safety standards to ensure the cleaning is done effectively and efficiently.

In this category, we take care of High access window cleaning, Hygiene services, Chemical & sewer deep cleaning, Industrial cleaning & Pest management

Our high access and window cleaning service is performed with the help of specialised equipment including ladders, scaffolding, water-fed pole systems and mobile elevating work platforms to ensure effective and safe cleaning of the surface.
